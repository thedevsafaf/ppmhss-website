About Us

PPM Higher Secondary School was established in 1976. It is run by Nediyiruppu Muslim Educational Association set up for the upliftment of the backward in Educational, Cultural and Social Spheres. The Association is an organization registered under Societies Registration Act 1860. (No S.22/75).

PPM Higher Secondary School is situated 2 KM away from Kondotty and is at stones throw distance away from the National Highway 966 of Kozhikkode-Palakkad. In its germinal stage it had three divisions with 112 strundents. In course of years it has surprisingly sprawled to cater the educational needs of 4200 students with 71 divisions. The Higher Secondary section was started in the year 2000-2001 with three batches, ie. Two Science (Biology)batches and one Humanities (Statistics) batch in the year 2002-2003. It was broadended its strength with three additional bataches, ie, Computer Science, Sociology and Commerce(Computer Application) respectively. In 2011-2012 it broadened again with two additional batches,ie, One Science (Biology), Commerce (Computer Application). At HigherScondary level there are thousand students. The school has coveted track records both at Curricular and Co-curricular level to testify its name and fame.

PPM Higher Secondary school has been declared as Minority Education Institution with the meaning of section 2(B) of the national Commission For Minority Education Act 2004, covered under article 30 of the constitution of India as per F 2186 of 2010-50594 dated 22/02/2011.

Our Values

MISSION:
“To promote value-based education, helping the students to discover their aptitudes and to realize their potential by boosting their self-confidence and creative-thinking skills.To provide an inclusive and harmonious educational atmosphere for all students of all communities and social strata.To inculcate excellent moral and ethical values in adolescents.”

VISION:
“Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practise the core values of the school: respect, tolerance & inclusion, and excellence.”
